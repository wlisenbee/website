Integrated Hydrologic Systems Lab




Dr. Whitney Lisenbee is the PI of Integrated Hydrologic Systems Lab at the University of Georgia. She holds an assistant professor position in the School of Civil, Environmental, Agricultural and Mechanical Engineering

CV Bio

Our group combines field and modeling studies to investigate the hydrology and water quality of “integrated hydrologic systems” which bring together expertise in both urban, agricultural, and natural landscapes.
